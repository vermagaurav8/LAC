Lucknow University

University of Lucknow is public authorised university founded in 1867 with a motto of Light and Learning located in capital city of Uttar Pradesh. Lucknow University established in 1920 with a wide campus area of 220 acres with over more than 160 affiliated collages and 1,00,000+ students, the university offers undegraduate, postgraduate and research courses in various stream such as Arts, Science, Commerce, Law and Fine Arts. It is affiliated to University Grants Commission in 1921. 



Faculty of Law, LU

The Faculty of law, University of Lucknow is a pioneer legal institution established in 1921. The real architect was Prof. R.U.Singh with keenness and enthusiasm organized teaching and research in the faculty in the systematic way. The faculty has produced many legal personalities including former President of India, Supreme Court and High Court judges, Law makers and etc. The faculty include expert legal member and bright student with brilliant minds. The Faculty is running a centre of Excellence on Gandhian dream of Litigation Free India. The Law Faculty is seprated from amin campus and shifted to the new campus having a total area of more than 75 acres located on Sitapur Road, Lucknow from 2002-03. 



Legal Aid Clinic

Legal aid clinic is an Initiative of the Faculty of Law, University of Lucknow with the determination to promote 'EQUAL ACCESS TO JUSTICE'. The centre is a specialized organ of the faculty of law which is playing active role in providing legal aid to the public at large. It tend to provide the free legal aidd to those unprivileged, marginalised or deprived class of Society. The centre was established with the objective to promote legal awareness by the means of pro-bono program like consultation camps , workshops , seminars, Menstrual Hygiene and cleanliness drives , Legal Aid and awareness sessions and legal literacy programs . The centre invites various practicing advocates and legal acadmecians to render their support to the indigent .  The Legal Aid Clinic has collaboration with District Legal Services Authority of Lucknow , other  social institution and other NGO of Lucknow. The services of our centre is accessible to all seven days of week with timing of 2:00 pm to 6:00 pm.
